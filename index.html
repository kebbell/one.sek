About

A

Welcome to the world of one sek — DJ, producer, and avid vinyl collector with a passion for preserving the soul of dance music.

As an artist and selector, I spend my time digging through crates, hunting down rare vinyl, and curating mixes that capture the raw energy of the dancefloor. Whether it's spinning the classics or showcasing the future of underground sounds, I’m dedicated to keeping the spirit of 90s club culture alive through every track I play.

On my page, you’ll find DJ mixes, live radio shows, and glimpses of my vinyl collection. From recent digs to upcoming projects, I’ll share my journey and the tracks that keep me moving. You can also catch details about my future events and exclusive guest mixes from other artists who inspire me.

Stay tuned for new releases, show dates, and deeper dives into my record collection — this is just the beginning of the ride.
